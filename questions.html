What are the devices

We use a set of devices to communicate emotions between you and your remote partner by changing elements of your environment like light color and sound.

Each person receives a set of three devices:

Master

Send emotions through touch or press

Light

Displays emotions through changes in light color

Sound

Plays sounds that represent emotions

How do the devices work

Communication between you and your partner happens only through the master devices because it is connected to the light and sound devices and controls how they respond.

Personalization is also handled through the master device. It receives signals from the website to store your preferred light colors and sounds for each emotion.

In order to send an emotion, follow the following steps:

1

Both parties need to be present in the same space. (A white light indicator of the master device will lit up)

2

Press on the master device to see selection of emotion. Release on the emotion you want to send.

3

Emotion will be displayed on both you and your partner's devices.
